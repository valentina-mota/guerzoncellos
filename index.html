BIOGRAPHY

The duo is unique worldwide in both formation and repertoire. The musical genres performed by GuerzonCellos are extensive, ranging from Baroque to jazz and rock, further enriched by their own compositions. Exceptional energy meets virtuosity, making their concerts a truly unique experience.

The Bolognese duo is composed of Tiziano Guerzoni and Enrico Guerzoni, son and father, who not only play the cello in a way rarely heard before but celebrate their instrument as an incredibly eclectic vehicle for musical expression. Fierce interpretations of Baroque classics and creative rearrangements of jazz and rock pieces turn their concerts into a highly sophisticated performance.

The peak of their technical and creative abilities is found in their own compositions, where Tiziano and Enrico blend musical influences from the 18th century with psychedelic elements, jazz, improvisations, and traditional folk. GuerzonCellos have performed in Italy, France, Austria, and China, and have released two CDs: the first, GuerzonCellos in 2015, and the second one iClown in 2021, through Da Vinci Publishing (Osaka Jp.).
